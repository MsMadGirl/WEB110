My career goals

I am pursuing a certificate in Database Administration and Design, with a secondary specialty in Web Development. I intend to be a back-end developer, but am studying front-end web dev in order to pursue a personal project as well. That project is called Red in the Ledger, and will be an ungendered hormone cycle tracker. This is intended solely as a side project, not as a future start-up.

My interests

In addition to programming, I am involved in textile arts. I am a spinner, weaver, and needle-felter, and am currently exploring e-textiles using LilyPad Arduinos. I also write non-fiction, and have self-published a slim volume of poetry entitled A Litany for the Many Dead. I am a former cook and restauranteur, having owned an all-night establishment called The Night Kitchen in Downtown Seattle. I have a dog and four cats.
